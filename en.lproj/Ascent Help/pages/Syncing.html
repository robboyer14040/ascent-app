Syncing to a GPS

Ascent currently supports downloading from GARMIN USB devices, such as the Edge 305.

To sync to your GPS:

  1. Connect the GPS to your computer. You don't have to turn it on.
  2. Start Ascent (if it isn't already running), and go to a browser windw
  3. Choose "Sync to GPS..." from the file menu, or press CMD-L
  4. When the sync is complete, new activities should appear in the browser, if any were downloaded
